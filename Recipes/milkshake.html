MilkShake

A vanilla milkshake recipe that's very easy to make. Add chocolate, berries, peaches, or whatever you like. My kids like to add root beer instead of milk.

The ingredients:

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

How to make Milkshake Step-By-Step


  1. Step 1:

    2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth; pour into glasses and serve.
